دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٨

Jazm

Jazm ْ is a new sign. The pupil is required to identify this sign in the following exercise.

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٩

After learning the sound of a letter with Fatha, Kasra or Damma, the pupil now learns, how this sound is blended with the next letter, which has jazm on it. Jazm with Fatha is given first.

Example: أَب is 'ab', جَد is 'jad', etc.
