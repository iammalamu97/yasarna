فَاعِدَة ٣٤ — مُكَمِّل

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ١٧

The two signs ـٓ or ـٓ are called Madda. The first one is called short Madda and the second one is long Madda. The pupil is asked to identify them in the following.

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ١٨

When Madda ـٓ or ـٓ is placed on any letter, the sound of that letter is prolonged.
