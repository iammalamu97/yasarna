Blank Letters

A blank letter is one which is without any sign. It is not pronounced, yet it is written down. (However every blank letter is without any sign. A blank Aliph with Fatha on a letter before it, and no Jazm after it, is not silent. It is pronounced according to lesson No. 10.)

Note: If the letter 'ya' is written without its two dots it is also silent. In the examples below, Arabic equivalents have been shown in smaller prints.
