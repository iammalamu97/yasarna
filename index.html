بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

نَحْمَدُهُ وَنُصَلِّي عَلَىٰ رَسُولِهِ الْكَرِيمِ

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ١

The dot

A dot has been given below. By placing a finger beside it the child is told that this is a dot (Nuqta).

Here the child is made to count the number of dots at a place.
He is instructed to count from the right.

The child should identify whether the given dots are above or below the line.
(He should start from the right as before.)
