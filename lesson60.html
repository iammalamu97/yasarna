Description of Salat (Prayer)

The importance of prayer in Islam is great as it is the foremost duty of Muslims and one of the Pillars on which the structure of Islam stands. It distinguishes Muslims from non-Muslims. The necessary things regarding the salat are described in the chart below.

Name of Salat No. of Rakats Timings
Fard Wajib Sunnah Nafl Time begins Time ends
Fajr 2 . 2 Nafl is prohibited at this time Breaking of the dawn Till Sunrise
Zuhr 4 . 61 2 After the Sun declines from its Zenith Until it is about midway from setting
Asr 4 . 4 before Fard Nafl is prohibited after Asr Soon after the time of Zuhr prayer ends Just before Sunset
Maghrib 3 . 2 2 Just after Sunset Extends to a period of an hour and a half
Isha 4 3 witr 62 4 About an hour and a half after Sunset Extends to dawn
Juma 2 . 103 2 Same as Zuhr Same as Zuhr
Eid Prayers . 2 . . From Sunrise Till Zuhr

1. 4 sunnat before fard and 2 sunnat after Fard, then 2 nafl.
2. 4 sunnat, then 4 fard, then 2 sunnat, then 2 nafl, then 3 witr, then 2 nafl. The time of witr is from Isha till the time for Isha prayer.
3. 4 sunnat before khutba, 4 sunnat after Juma, then 2 sunnat, then 2 nafl.
