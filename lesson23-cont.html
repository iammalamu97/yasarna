فَاعِدَة ٢٣ — مُكَمِّل

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ١٠

There are three full vowels among the Arabic alphabets. These are — Aliph ا, Vao و and ya ي. The rest are consonants. If there is a consonant letter with sign Fatha, Kasra and Damma and after it is a vowel, which is either blank or with Jazm, the sound of the consonant letter is modified.
