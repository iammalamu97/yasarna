Wuqoof - Stops

The stop signs

Non stopping at ◌: In this case, the sign is regarded to be just ل, which means do not stop. One has to proceed on to the next word without pause, connecting the next word if need be.

Stopping at ◌: In this case, the sign is regarded to be just ◌, meant for stopping.

Beginning the next word after ◌: In here, there are three cases — (1) If the word next to ◌ begins with Shadda, the Shadda is replaced by Fatha. Illustrations of not stopping and stopping at ◌ are given below.
