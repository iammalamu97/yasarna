دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٢

Single Letters

In this lesson, alphabets (letters) are being introduced. The pupil is required to pronounce the name of the alphabet as he reads from right to left. If at any stage he is stuck or makes a mistake, as for example he reads a letter as 'ba' whereas it is as 'ta' as it has two dots above the line, and so forth. In this way, he will be able to connect the form of the alphabet with its name which is very necessary. But otherwise, there is no need to refer to dots or to their positions while teaching a child. He must always read in a flow.
