دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٣٧

Wuqoof - Stops

(3) If the word after ◌ begins with a blank Aliph or with a Nun-Qutni, but there is no Lam after it, the word is then started with a sign, same as that of the 3rd letter after ◌. Illustrations of not stopping and stopping at ◌ are given below.

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٣٨

After vertical Kasra, if there is a blank bend, the vertical Kasra changes to prolonged Kasra.

Example: مَجرِهَا is read as مَجرِيهَا (Majreha). It is not read as 'Majriha'. There is only one example of this kind in the Holy Quran.

دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٣٩

Small 'seen or nun'

A. Small print 'seen' س is sometimes placed on top of the letter ص. The sound of either letter may be pronounced.

Examples: بِصط may also be pronounced بِسط  |  بَصطَة may also be pronounced بَسطَة  |  المُصيطِرُون may also be pronounced المُسيطِرُون  |  بِمُصيطِر may also be pronounced بِمُسيطِر
