دَرْسٌ نَمْبَر ٢٩

Tanween before Shadda

The normal sound of Tanween is not produced if there is a shadda immediately after it. There are two situations: (a) Tanween changes to Fatha, Kasra or Damma as the case may be, if shadda is upon the consonant. (b) Tanween changes to half (Nun Ghunna) if the Shadda is on a vowel.

ثَاوَّ is read 'Ti(n)wwa'. It is not read 'Tinwwa' nor Tiwwa.
