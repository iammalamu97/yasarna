دَرْسٌ نَمْبَرَه

Kasra

In this lesson the pupil learns what sound a letter produces when it is given the sign of Kasra.

(Note: Example in kasra have purposely been given before those of Fatha.)

Example: The sound of بِ is 'bi'; تِ is 'ti'; لِ is 'li'.

Here the letters are no more pronounced with their original name. As before, the pupil is required to read in a flow.
