Muqatte'at Letters of abbreviation in Quran

Pronunciation of the Muqatte'at Letters is governed by these rules: (1) A letter bearing Madda is read by its original name with prolonged sound, (2) A letter with vertical Fatha is pronounced according to its sign, (3) Blank Aliph, here, is not silent. It is read by its original name. (4) A letter with Shadda combines the letter that comes before it according to common rule.
